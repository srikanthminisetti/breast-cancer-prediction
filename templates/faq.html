Breast cancer is a disease in which abnormal cells in the breast grow uncontrollably and form tumors. If untreated, these tumors can spread to other parts of the body.

Breast cancer can be caused by a combination of genetic, hormonal, environmental, and lifestyle factors. Family history and age may increase risk.

You should consult a primary care physician, gynecologist, or oncologist for professional medical advice.

Regular physical activity can help maintain a healthy weight and reduce the risk of breast cancer by balancing hormone levels.
