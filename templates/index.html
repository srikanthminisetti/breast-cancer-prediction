India's Hotspots

Breast cancer is the most prevalent cancer among women in India and a leading cause of cancer-related deaths.The number of breast cancer cases in India is projected to rise to over 2.3 lakh by 2025.

  • The average estimated breast cancer incidence in India is 31.3 cases per 1 lakh women (2020).
